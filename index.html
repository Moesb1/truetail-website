About Us

Hi, we're TrueTail.
Here's our story.

TrueTail started with a simple, stubborn belief: the dogs and cats who give us their whole hearts deserve treats made with the same honesty we'd want on our own plate. No fillers hiding behind a science-y name, no "flavors" that are really just chemistry. Just chicken, sweet potato, oats, egg, olive oil — ingredients you can picture, from a kitchen you could actually stand in.

Every batch is small, homemade, and sealed in a resealable stand-up pouch built to keep things fresh — not because it looks good on a shelf (though, honestly, it does), but because your dog's treats deserve the same freshness you'd want for your own snacks.
